The Purge of Alcaire

The Purge of Alcaire marked a dark chapter for the Assassins Brotherhood (TAB). The relentless siege led by the Grand Templar Order (GTO) resulted in more fatalities than the Brotherhood had ever faced. The once-thriving town of Alcaire, known for its strategic importance and its close ties to the Brotherhood, became a battleground of devastation. The Templars' calculated assault, led by the ruthless Grand Master Arkylos the Herald, caught the Assassins off guard, leading to a massacre that would be etched into the memory of every survivor.

Morale within the Brotherhood was at an all-time low, particularly when their own leader, Snatchup, chose to hide rather than defend the town. Snatchup’s decision to retreat behind fortified walls instead of leading his people in the defense of Alcaire was seen as an act of cowardice by many. This choice not only resulted in significant loss of life but also caused a significant rift within the Brotherhood, leading many to feel hopeless and abandoned. The once unbreakable spirit of the Assassins was shattered, and trust in their leadership was severely undermined.

Despite their dire circumstances, the Assassins Council resolutely refused to negotiate with the Templars. They understood that any form of negotiation would ultimately be used against them to overturn their ideologies. This defiant stance was rooted in the belief that compromising with the Templars would betray the very principles the Brotherhood stood for—freedom and resistance against tyranny. The Council’s unwavering resolve, however, came at a steep cost.

In response to the Assassins' refusal to negotiate, the GTO adopted brutal tactics to instill fear and suppress resistance. Widespread torture of both Assassins and civilians became the norm. Prisoners were subjected to inhumane conditions, coerced into revealing secrets about the Brotherhood’s operations, and used as examples to deter others from opposing the Templars. The once bustling streets of Alcaire were now filled with the echoes of suffering and despair, as the Templars sought to break the will of the Assassins and the townspeople alike.

The GTO's harsh treatment of prisoners, however, backfired. The extreme measures taken by the Templars caused fear and unrest among the people, leading many to question the legitimacy and morality of the Templar Order. Seeking safety and protection, many civilians turned to the Assassins Brotherhood for refuge. This influx of new members allowed the Brotherhood to regrow their numbers tenfold. The suffering inflicted by the Templars forged a bond between the Assassins and the people, rekindling hope and strengthening their resolve to fight against their oppressors.

In the aftermath of the raid, KingOfGenres, the prior leader before Snatchup, reclaimed the position of Mentor. Vowing to never allow such a catastrophe to happen again, KingOfGenres focused on rebuilding the Brotherhood’s strength and unity. His return was marked by a renewed sense of purpose and determination. He addressed the rift within the Brotherhood by reaffirming the principles of their creed and emphasizing the importance of solidarity in the face of adversity.

Under his leadership, the Assassins adopted new strategies and fortified their hidden enclaves, ensuring they would be better prepared for future conflicts. KingOfGenres implemented rigorous training programs to enhance the skills of both new recruits and veteran Assassins. He also prioritized the reconstruction and fortification of their safe houses, creating a network of secure locations where the Brotherhood could regroup and plan their operations.
