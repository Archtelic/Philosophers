The Creation of Sentinels

Shortly after the brutal Purge of Alcaire, KingOfGenres reclaimed the position of "Mentor" within the Creed. The battle had left the Brotherhood in disarray, and it took considerable time for things to settle down. Unfortunately, during this period of recovery, the Templars had already expanded their influence, building encampments alarmingly close to the Assassins' remaining forts. The close proximity of these encampments sowed seeds of fear among the people. The blood of those who fell in Alcaire still lingered in the air, a grim reminder of the Templars' ruthlessness.

Fear and Unrest

The fear among the populace was palpable. The memories of the Purge were still fresh, and the presence of Templar encampments so near their homes and fortresses made the people uneasy. The Templars, sensing this fear, attempted a covert operation to assassinate the Assassins' new leader, KingOfGenres. They knew that if King were to fall, victory would be within their grasp. Underestimating King's capabilities, they only sent a small but fierce group. These men, clad in black attire unlike the Templars' traditional wears, infiltrated the city under the cover of darkness.

The Attempt on King’s Life

The attempt on King’s life was swift and brutal. The Templars moved silently, striking without warning. But King was not easily taken by surprise. His years of training and experience had honed his senses to a razor's edge. When the attackers struck, King fought back with a ferocity that belied his calm exterior. The battle was intense, but in the end, King emerged victorious, his would-be assassins lying dead at his feet. The failed attempt only served to strengthen his resolve.

Formation of the Sentinels

Realizing the need for greater security and protection, King decided to form a new division within the Assassins. This group, known as the Sentinels, was tasked with both protecting the city and safeguarding its people. The creation of the Sentinels calmed the public, restoring a sense of safety and order. Many who had lived their lives with ideals of justice eagerly joined this new division, inspired by the bravery and determination of KingOfGenres.

Leadership and Organization

To ensure the effectiveness of the Sentinels, King placed a trusted member of his council, NativeGage, in charge. Promoting him to the position of Commodore, Gage was tasked with organizing and leading the Sentinels. Gage embraced his new role with dedication and skill, drawing on his experience and wisdom to build a strong and disciplined force. Under his leadership, the Sentinels became a formidable presence, patrolling the streets and fortifying the defenses.

Growth and Evolution

The Sentinels quickly proved their worth. Their presence deterred further Templar incursions and reassured the people. As time went on, the division grew in strength and numbers. Each new Commodore brought fresh ideas and improvements, refining the strategies and tactics of the Sentinels. The bond between the Assassins and the people deepened, with many civilians volunteering to aid in the protection of their city.

Legacy of the Sentinels

The creation of the Sentinels marked a turning point for the Assassins Brotherhood. It demonstrated their ability to adapt and evolve in the face of adversity. KingOfGenres' leadership and vision had not only restored order but had also forged a new path forward. The Sentinels became a symbol of hope and resilience, embodying the ideals of justice and protection.

NativeGage, as the first Commodore, left a lasting legacy. His efforts laid the foundation for future leaders, each of whom built upon his work to create an even stronger and more effective division. The Sentinels continued to protect the city and its people, standing as a testament to the unyielding spirit of the Assassins Brotherhood.

A New Dawn

With the city secure and the people united, KingOfGenres looked to the future with renewed determination. The Assassins had endured much, but they had emerged stronger. The Purge of Alcaire had been a dark chapter, but it had also sparked a new era of resilience and unity. Under King’s guidance, the Brotherhood continued to thrive, ever vigilant against the threats that lay beyond their walls.

The Assassins knew that their fight was far from over, but with the Sentinels standing guard and the people rallying behind them, they faced the future with hope and confidence. The legacy of Morsera and Alcaire lived on, a reminder of their enduring commitment to freedom and justice.
