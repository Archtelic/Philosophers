THE CHASSEUR-BLACK CROSS

Before ascending to the esteemed position of Seneschal and later Grand Master, Aezerix's journey within the Templar Order was marked by unwavering loyalty and steadfast dedication. Aezerix arrived in the New World initially as a member of the Chasseurs Reinforcement Force sent by the King of France.
As a young recruit, Aezerix displayed exceptional skills in both combat and strategic thinking. His dedication caught the eye of Grand Master Arkylos and later, Skibbo, who recognized the potential for greatness within the aspiring Templar. Aezerix quickly rose through the ranks, earning the respect and admiration of his peers. Under the tutelage of the Original Inner Sanctum, Aezerix learned the art of leadership and the importance of maintaining the delicate balance between strength and compassion.
Aezerix joined the Order of the Black Cross and became the third Knight Commander of the Black Cross. During his short reign as Knight Commander, Aezerix managed to instigate a brief age of chaos in the lands of The Assassin’s Brotherhood by launching a planned surprise attack on the Assassin Sentinels' stronghold. They succeeded in massacring a large number of assassins and imprisoned the remaining ones, subjecting them to torture. This incident, contrary to the elusive nature of the Black Cross, led to their relocation to the western regions of the New World for an unrelated espionage mission. Until the middle of Grand Master Aezerix's reign, they were not sighted again.
THE UNWAVERING SENESCHAL
As Seneschal, Aezerix served as Skibbo's right hand, overseeing the day-to-day operations of the Templar Order. His strategic acumen and organizational skills were crucial in maintaining the faction's stability during Skibbo’s absence. During Seneschal Aezerix’s term, Skibbo frequently returned to the Old World Nations to request reinforcements. During this period, the Templars faced external threats and internal strife, yet Aezerix's steady hand and unyielding determination became the glue that held the faction together.

However, fate took a dark turn when the Templar Grand Master was killed. Skibbo fell victim to an ambush by two dozen assassins on his ship. Aezerix, witnessing the tragic demise of his Grand Master, felt the weight of the Templar legacy squarely on his shoulders.

In the aftermath of Skibbo's death, Aezerix, driven by grief and a sense of duty, assumed the mantle of Grand Master. He vowed to honor the old Grand Masters' memories by steering the Templars through the storm of chaos and rebuilding the faction stronger than ever before. Aezerix's leadership, forged in the crucible of adversity, became the guiding light that led the Templar Order to a second Golden Age, marking a new chapter in their storied history.
